Dear Customer:

 

As it is essential to your long-term planning to anticipate any changes in the lifecycle status of your medical equipment, we wanted to notify you in advance of the key milestones for your product(s).

 

The end-of-life (EOL) and end-of-support (EOS) milestones for one or more of your Philips system(s) is listed below.

EOL signals the end of the following:

 

•

Full operational support including sustaining engineering or upgrade development

 

•

Economic maintenance, guaranteed availability of service parts, and/or technical/help desk support

 

At EOL, any service activity can only be completed under Limited Support conditions. After the above date, our ability to reliably maintain the system to its original specifications may be compromised, possibly resulting in elevated operational and cybersecurity risks, and vulnerability to threats and disruptions.

 

EOS signals the following:

 

•

Our ability to meet your service needs could be impacted due to unavailability of technology upgrades, parts, and trained service personnel.

 

•

Our intention to continue to support your system(s) until the EOS date, on a commercially reasonable basis, with limitations on renewing/extending service contracts.

 

Please contact your Account Manager to identify a solution that best fits your business requirements. If you have immediate questions or concerns, or if the systems listed above are no longer in service, please call us at 1-800-229-6417. You can also check the Philips Customer Services Portal (www.customerservices.philips.com) for updates on the life cycle status.
